A leopard is a large, powerful, and very adaptable wild cat. It is one of the most famous big cats in the world because of its beautiful spotted skin and hunting skills. Main Details about Leopard 🐆 Scientific Name: Panthera pardus Family: Felidae (cat family) Appearance: Golden-yellow fur with black rosettes (spots shaped like roses). They are smaller than lions and tigers but very strong. Males are larger than females.



The tiger 🐅 is the largest member of the cat family and one of the most powerful predators in the world. It is well known for its striking orange coat with black stripes. Main Details about Tiger: Scientific Name: Panthera tigris Family: Felidae (cat family) Appearance: Orange or reddish coat with black vertical stripes. White fur on the belly and inner parts. Each tiger has a unique stripe pattern, like human fingerprint



The bear 🐻 is a large, strong mammal found in different parts of the world. Bears are known for their bulky bodies, thick fur, and great strength. There are several species of bears, each with unique features. General Information about Bears: Scientific Family: Ursidae Appearance: Large body with thick fur.
